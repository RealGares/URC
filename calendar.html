Race Information

Race Schedule

All races follow a standard schedule to help drivers plan their participation:

  • 12:00 PM AEST (Friday): Announcement is made for driver check-in
  • 6:00 PM AEST (Monday): Mandatory drivers' briefing
  • 6:35 PM AEST (Monday): Lobby closes and qualifying begins
  • 6:55 PM AEST (Monday) (approx): Race start

Race Format

Each round includes the following components:

  • Qualifying: 17 minute qualifying session
  • Race: 50% race distance, no formation lap
  • Weather: Dynamic conditions
  • Damage: Standard simulation

Special Events

Throughout the season, special events add variety to the championship:

  • Non-Championship Round: Experimental format with reverse grids at Zandvoort
  • Break Weeks: Often feature special community events
  • Season Finale: Double points for our Abu Dhabi finale
  • Post-Season: All-Star exhibition race featuring the season's top drivers
