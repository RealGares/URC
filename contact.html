Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join the URC racing league?
To join URC, simply fill out the registration form on our signup page. After submitting your application, our team will review it and reach out within 48 hours with further instructions. You'll need to complete a brief assessment to determine your division placement.
How do I report technical issues during a race?
If you experience technical issues during a race, immediately notify the Race Director via the Discord server. For connection problems, we recommend restarting your game and rejoining. After the race, submit a detailed description of the issue through our Discord support channel.
How can I submit an incident for steward review?
To report a racing incident, use the incident reporting form on our Discord server within 24 hours of the race. Include the lap number, corner, round, and drivers involved, along with video evidence from your perspective. Our stewards panel will review all submitted incidents and provide decisions before the next race.
How can I become a commentator or stream races?
We're always looking for talented commentators and content creators. If you're interested in joining our broadcast team, please contact "jonno_aus" on Discord. For personal streaming, all drivers are permitted to stream their races, with optional URC branding. Make sure to follow our content guidelines.
