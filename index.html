About URC

The Ultimate Racing Challenge

URC is the premier F1 racing league of the 2025 season, bringing together the most talented virtual drivers from around the world to compete in a high-stakes championship.

Founded in 2025, our league has quickly become known for its professional organization, competitive racing, and vibrant community. We use the latest F1 25 game to create an authentic racing experience that mirrors the real-world Formula 1 championship.

With races held weekly on custom-tailored circuits and a comprehensive scoring system, URC offers a complete racing experience for both competitors and spectators.
